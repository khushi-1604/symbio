                      Our Story
                    

                  

                  

                     is a rapidly expanding agritech solutions provider in India and has been spotlighted by Business Today under the “Top 500 Growth Leaders in Emerging Markets.” The company is listed with both the Bombay Stock Exchange and the National Stock Exchange of India. Recognized as the “Innovator of the Year” by the Indian Agricultural Research Alliance at the Global Agritech Forum 2019, AgroNova has received several accolades from industry organizations. Recently, the company achieved the “Best Workplace in AgriTech 2019–20” certification. With a strong footprint across India, it operates through regional hubs in every key agricultural zone. The company boasts 4 advanced manufacturing plants, 42 regional warehouses, and 10 zonal headquarters that support a distribution ecosystem of 7,800 dealers and over 72,000 local retailers. AgroNova’s dedicated team of 1,200+ agronomists and commercial professionals, backed by its ISO-certified innovation center and a web of global partnerships with six major agricultural biotech firms across the US, Germany, and South Korea, ensures cutting-edge solutions for over 9 million Indian cultivators annually.
